Tom Yum

Tom yum is Thailand's most popular soup and there's some pretty stiff competition in that category. It's spin- tinglingly sour, spicy and steaming with fragrant aromatics.



INGREDIENTS


  • Chicken (sliced or shredded)
  • Lemongrass (stalks, bruised)
  • Kaffir lime leaves (fresh or dried)
  • Galangal (sliced)
  • Thai bird's eye chilies
  • Straw mushrooms (whole or sliced)
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
  • Thai basil leaves (for garnish)



PROCEDURES


  1. Simmer sliced or shredded chicken, bruised lemongrass, sliced galangal, kaffir lime leaves, sliced Thai bird's eye chilies, and straw mushrooms in water.

  2. Season the soup with fish sauce.

  3. Add lime juice to taste.

  4. Serve the soup hot, garnished with fresh cilantro and Thai basil leaves.
